Privacy Policy for RoadLink

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to RoadLink ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We collect your real-time location data when you use RoadLink to:

  • Show your position on the map
  • Connect you with nearby drivers
  • Calculate distances to other users
  • Enable location-based features

You can control location sharing: You can go offline at any time to stop sharing your location.

2.2 User Information

We collect information you provide:

  • Display name
  • Vehicle registration (optional)
  • Visibility preferences

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, OS version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Messages sent and received
  • Time and duration of use

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Connect you with nearby drivers
  • Enable real-time communication
  • Improve user experience
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 With Other Users

When you're online, we share with nearby users:

  • Your approximate location (rounded for privacy)
  • Your display name
  • Your vehicle registration (if you enabled this)
  • Messages you send

4.2 We Do NOT Share

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights
  • Prevent fraud
  • Protect user safety

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access

Note: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Location data: Deleted after 24 hours
  • Messages: Deleted after 7 days
  • Account data: Retained until account deletion

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Object: Object to certain data processing
  • Portability: Receive your data in portable format

8. Children's Privacy

RoadLink is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to withdraw consent
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority
  • Right to data portability

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising rights

  • Analytics cookies: To improve the service (can be disabled)
